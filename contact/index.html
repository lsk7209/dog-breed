Contact BreedWise

Contact BreedWise about dog breed planning content, corrections, source questions, or editorial feedback.

For corrections, source suggestions, or editorial feedback, contact the DogBreedCost editorial desk. The public repository remains available for transparent source and page-history review.

Contact channel

Use editor@dogbreedcost.com for corrections and source questions. You can also use the repository page at github.com/lsk7209/dog-breed when a public issue is useful.

What to include

Please include the page URL, the sentence you are questioning, and the source you believe should be reviewed. For broken links, include the replacement URL if you know one.

Correction priorities

BreedWise prioritizes corrections that affect health-risk wording, cost assumptions, source links, disclosure language, or statements that might be mistaken for veterinary or financial advice.

Response expectations

This is a small static site, so response times may vary. Urgent pet health questions should go to a licensed veterinarian or emergency veterinary service, not to BreedWise.
