Advertising and Affiliate Disclosure

BreedWise disclosure page for advertising, affiliate links, editorial independence, and health-content limits.

BreedWise may display ads or affiliate links in the future. Advertising relationships should not determine health-risk statements, article conclusions, or breed planning guidance.

Advertising policy

Ads, if enabled, may appear through third-party advertising platforms. Ad placement does not mean BreedWise endorses an advertiser, product, insurer, breeder, or service.

Affiliate policy

If affiliate links are added, they should be labeled near the affected link or page. BreedWise does not sell rankings and does not present affiliate payouts as evidence.

Editorial independence

Sponsored placements, if added later, must be labeled. Editorial pages should continue to cite sources and explain limits even when monetization exists elsewhere on the site.

Health-content limit

Articles are educational and do not replace veterinary care. BreedWise does not provide diagnosis, treatment instructions, prescription guidance, or emergency triage.

Financial-content limit

Cost ranges are planning estimates and public-study context. They are not insurance quotes, provider recommendations, or financial advice.
