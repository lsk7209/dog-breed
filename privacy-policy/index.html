Privacy Policy

BreedWise privacy policy for a static informational dog breed planning site.

BreedWise is currently a static informational website. It does not provide accounts, payments, comments, or forms.

Information we collect

BreedWise does not intentionally collect personal information through this static site. Server logs may be created by the hosting provider and can include standard technical information such as IP address, browser, device, referring page, and time of visit.

Cookies and advertising

If Google AdSense or another advertising product is enabled, advertising partners may use cookies or similar technologies to serve and measure ads. Google may use cookies to personalize or limit ads depending on user settings, consent requirements, and applicable law. Users can review Google ad settings at adssettings.google.com.

Analytics

If analytics are added later, BreedWise should update this policy to identify the provider, the purpose of measurement, and any available opt-out choices.

External links

BreedWise links to veterinary, university, breed-club, regulatory, and commercial cost-study resources for context. Those sites have their own privacy practices.

Children

BreedWise is intended for general audiences researching dog ownership. It does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Contact

Questions or correction requests can be sent through the public repository linked on the contact page.
